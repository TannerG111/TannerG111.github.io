  3. Design

    3. CRAP
      1. Contrast

        Contrast is colors makes text readable, and contrast between headings and text size helps them stand out. Interactive parts such as buttons also stand out with different shapes and colors. I think some more contrast could be implemented with a unique color for certain elements or sections to help break up the pages as currently everything appears to be white or a shade of green. Also the "Learn CSS with Angel" section on the index page has a light green background on the normal white background which I do not think has enough contrast to stand out from the background.

      2. Repetition

        There is a clear design scheme for the site, with the same colors and fonts used throughout the site. Elements are also formatted in a consistent manner, with the same padding and margins used throughout the site for elemtns such as paragraphs and similar sections.

      3. Alignment

        For the most part elements are aligned to the left giving a clear feeling of organization and alignment, with appropriate margins and padding to keep alignment but add visual interest. There are a couple of elements that are centered such as the "Learn CSS with Angel" section on the index page and the animated block on the "Animations" page which feel out of place due to the other elements being left aligned.

      4. Proximity

        The proximity of elements is good with related content being grouped together and other content being separated with headings. There is a lot of white space on some pages due to a lack of content, also causing the footer to be in the middle of the page which is not visually appealing.
