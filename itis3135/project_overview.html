Project Overview

Project Name

Independence Lodge Website

Application Summary

The application will be a website for a local hotel. This website will be used to showcase the business and its rooms to draw business to the hotel.

Intended Users

The intended users are potential customers and guests of the hotel. They will use this site to preview the hotel, rooms, and location to determine if the hotel is a good fit for them.

Website Content Overview

  • Homepage of the hotel with a brief overview of the hotel and its rooms.
  • Rooms page with details about the rooms and their amenities.
  • Gallery page with photos of the hotel and its rooms.
  • Location page with a map, what is nearby, and directions to the hotel.
  • Contact page with contact information for the hotel and a form to contact the hotel.

Page Designs

Home

  • Purpose: Introduce the hotel and direct users to the rooms, gallery, location, and contact.
  • Audience: People looking for a hotel and wanting to learn more about the Independence Lodge
  • Content: A hero image of the hotel, a brief description of the hotel, slideshow of images of the hotel and rooms, contact information for the hotel, and a form to contact the hotel.
  • Data Entry: Yes, a contact form to contact the hotel.
  • Validations: Yes, to ensure the phone number and email are in the proper format.
  • Controls: Buttons to submit the contact form, next and previous buttons in the slideshow, and links in the header to the other pages.
  • Actions: The links will navigate to the other pages, next and previous buttons will change the image in the slideshow, and the contact form will submit the form to the hotel.

Rooms

  • Purpose: Talk about the hotel's rooms and amenities.
  • Audience: People interested in the hotel and wanting to learn more about the rooms and amenities.
  • Content: Room descriptions, included amentities, pricing, and a couple of photos of the rooms.
  • Data Entry: No.
  • Validations: No.
  • Controls: Links to the other pages.
  • Actions: The links will navigate to the other pages.

Gallery

  • Purpose: Let visitors review photos of the hotel and its rooms.
  • Audience: People interested in the hotel and wanting to see photos of the hotel and its rooms.
  • Content: A gallery of photos of the hotel and its rooms.
  • Data Entry: No.
  • Validations: N/A.
  • Controls: The images will enlarge when clicked on and have a next and previous button to navigate through the gallery, as well as links to the other pages.
  • Actions: The next and previous buttons will navigate to the next and previous image in the gallery, and the links will navigate to the other pages.

Location

  • Purpose: Provide information about the hotel's location, how to get there, and what is nearby.
  • Audience: People interested in the hotel and wanting to learn more about the location and what is nearby.
  • Content: Address, embedded map, directions to the hotel, and what is nearby.
  • Data Entry: No.
  • Validations: No.
  • Controls: Links to the other pages, a button to redirect to Google Maps directions to the hotel.
  • Actions: The links will navigate to the other pages, and the button will redirect to Google Maps directions to the hotel.

Contact

  • Purpose: Provide contact information for the hotel and a form to contact the hotel.
  • Audience: People interested in the hotel and wanting to contact the hotel.
  • Content: Address, email, phone, and a form to contact the hotel.
  • Data Entry: Yes.
  • Validations: Yes, to ensure the email and phone number are in the proper format.
  • Controls: Submit button for the form and links to the other pages.
  • Actions: The submit button will submit the form to the hotel, and the links will navigate to the other pages.

Interactivity Inspiration/References

The dynamic functionality of this website will include a contact form that will accept information from the user so they can easily contact the hotel, and a gallery that can have images clicked on to enlarge and navigate through the gallery for user convenience to view the images in a larger format. The contact form will be used in the contact page and likely the home page as well. The gallery will be used in the gallery page and the navigation piece will be utilized in the home page as well.
